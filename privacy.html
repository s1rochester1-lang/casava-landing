Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

Data we collect

We collect information you provide directly — business details, menu and pricing data, contact information — along with data generated as Casava operates on your behalf, such as customer inquiries, quotes, invoices and booking records.

How it's used

Data is used to run the automated workflow you've configured: replying to inquiries, generating quotes and invoices, processing payments, and scheduling events. We also use aggregated, anonymized data to improve the product.

Third-party services

Casava connects to services you choose to link — messaging providers, payment processors, and calendar tools. Data shared with these services is governed by their own privacy policies in addition to this one.

Your rights

You can request a copy of your data, ask us to correct it, or have it deleted at any time by contacting us. Deleting your account removes your configuration and stored records, subject to legal retention requirements.
