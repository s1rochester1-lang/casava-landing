Billing questions

Before you start.

What happens at day 30?

You'll be prompted to choose a paid plan. Nothing is charged automatically — your trial simply pauses until you decide.

What if I cancel?

Cancel anytime, no penalty. Your account stays active through the end of the current billing period.

Can I switch tiers later?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade whenever your business needs change, effective the next billing cycle.

Is there a setup fee?

No. There's never a setup fee on any tier.

Does the free trial include every feature?

The trial includes a core set of features, not every feature in every tier. Full details are confirmed at signup.
