What if I cancel?

Cancel anytime, no penalty. Your account stays active through the end of the current billing period.

Can I switch tiers later?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade whenever your business needs change, effective the next billing cycle.

Is there a setup fee?

No. There's never a setup fee on any tier.

Additional features are available when you upgrade to a paid plan.
