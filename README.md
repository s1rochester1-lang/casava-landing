# Casava — Catering Automation for Food Businesses

**Turn catering requests into confirmed bookings. Automatically.**

Casava handles the entire catering booking workflow — from the first inquiry to a paid, scheduled event — so restaurants, caterers, and food businesses can focus on the food, not the admin.

---

## What Casava Does

When a catering inquiry comes in, Casava takes it from request to confirmed booking without you lifting a finger. The entire process runs automatically. Your only job is a single tap to approve.

### The Six-Step Flow

| Step | Who Does It |
|---|---|
| Client submits a request form | Client |
| Quote built and sent in seconds | Casava |
| **Owner taps Approve** | **You — your one move** |
| Invoice created and sent instantly | Casava |
| Deposit, balance, and payment reminders handled | Casava |
| Event booked to calendar, confirmation sent | Casava |

Approve from WhatsApp, Telegram, SMS, or email — no new app to learn.

---

## The Problems Casava Solves

**Leads gone cold**
A client inquires at 11pm and books whoever replies first. Casava replies instantly, 24/7 — so you're always first, even while you sleep.

**The admin grind**
Quotes, invoices, calendar updates, payment chasing — dozens of minutes of paperwork per booking. Casava automates all of it after a single approval tap.

**Details falling through the cracks**
Guest counts, allergies, delivery addresses, timing — Casava captures every detail once at intake and carries it through automatically. No sticky notes, no missed calls, no dropped details.
